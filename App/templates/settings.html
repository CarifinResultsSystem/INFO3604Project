Our Mission

CariFin was created to solve a real problem — the lack of a centralised, reliable platform for Caribbean competition results. Too often, scores were scattered across spreadsheets, noticeboards, and word of mouth. We set out to change that.

Our goal is to make every result, ranking, and schedule instantly accessible to participants, coaches, institutions, and fans across the region — in real time, on any device.
